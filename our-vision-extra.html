Our Vision

We envision a thriving, Spirit-led community where every person discovers their identity in Christ, is equipped for ministry, and joyfully serves others. We aim to be a local expression of God's kingdom — inclusive, transformative, and outward-focused.

Key elements of our vision

  • Worship that is authentic, biblical and Spirit-empowered.
  • Discipleship pathways for all ages and life stages.
  • Community engagement and practical service to meet local needs.
  • Leadership development to raise faithful servant-leaders.
  • Partnerships across churches and organisations to multiply impact.

How we pursue this vision

We pursue our vision through regular worship, small groups, outreach projects, teaching programs, and mentoring. We prioritize prayer, pastoral care, and creating safe spaces for spiritual growth. Practically, this means resource-driven ministries, community events, and training opportunities that empower ordinary people to serve in extraordinary ways.

To learn how you can be part of this vision — joining a house group, volunteering, or partnering in outreach — please contact us via the Contact page or visit us at Wijkcentrum de Mortel.

Stichting Glory Tabernacle Parish is a multi-cultural church, Both young and old gather to worship God, irrespective of their background. We love you with the love of God.
