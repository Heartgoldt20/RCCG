Worship Experience

Are you searching for a place of worship in Eindhoven (Noord-Brabant)?
A church where you can experience God in person and grow spiritually in God’s purpose for your life?

Then you are welcome to Glory Tabernacle Parish.

During our service you will experience: Sunday School, anointed worship and praise, testimonies, and the undiluted Word of God through the leading of the Holy Spirit.

We trust God to give you a warm welcome and a life-changing experience.

Our Pastor

Welcome to Glory Tabernacle Parish. We are led by Pastor Dennis Shakka and Pastor Titilayo Shakka, who serve with a deep passion for God, His Word, and His people. Glory Tabernacle Parish is a Christ-centered church where the presence and power of God are ever present. Our mission is to raise lives that fulfill God’s will and purpose on earth, while keeping heaven as our ultimate goal. Through prayer, faith, and the life of Jesus Christ, we are committed to sharing the unconditional love of God and nurturing spiritual growth in every believer. You are warmly welcome to worship with us, grow with us, and encounter God in a deeper way. May God richly bless you. Our vision is to pursue holiness (Hebrews 12:14)
