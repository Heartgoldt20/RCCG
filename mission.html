Mission Statement

  • 1. To make heaven.
  • 2. To take as many people as possible with us.
  • 3. To have a member of the redeemed christain church of God family of all Nations.
  • 4. To accomplish No.1 Above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  • 5. To accomplish No.2&3 Above, We will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • 6. We will pursue these objectives until every nation in the world is reached for jesus christ our lord.

Glory Tabernacle is a multi-cultural church, Both young and old gather to worship God, irrespective of their background. We love you with the love of God.
