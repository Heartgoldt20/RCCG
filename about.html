About Glory Tabernacle Parish Eindhoven

Founded in 2007, RCCG Glory Tabernacle began with a small group of faithful believers and has grown into a vibrant spiritual home under the leadership of Pastor Dennis Shakka since 2014. Through challenges and transitions, our faith has remained unwavering, and our community continues to flourish.

Our History

The Redeemed Christian Church of God was founded in 1952, with a divine mission to spread the gospel worldwide. Inspired by a vision from God, Pastor Akindayomi established the church on a covenant of faithfulness and obedience to God's word. Today, RCCG continues to impact lives across the globe.
